Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to find those who are looking for a great time, and who appreciate the outdoors, and enable them to simply and easily find a worthy adventure for them.

Audience

We are looking for those who are adventures and always willing to try new things. Want to know what there is to do outside? GO rafting! Have you ever felt like the neighborhood swimming pool is lame (and not to mention gross)? GO Rafting! Ever wanted to feel the what it is like to sail a mighty storm? GO Rafting! (It's close enough. :)
